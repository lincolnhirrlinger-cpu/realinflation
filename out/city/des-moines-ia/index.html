Des Moines, Iowa

In Des Moines, Iowa, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.31 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.14 below the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $978.79, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.44% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Des Moines's cost-of-living index of 84 puts it below the national average of 100.
