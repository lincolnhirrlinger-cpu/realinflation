Phoenix, Arizona

In Phoenix, Arizona, a gallon of regular gas costs $4.54 as of 2026-03-23 — $1.09 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,519.02, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 3.32% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Phoenix's cost-of-living index of 100 puts it below the national average of 100.
