Tallahassee, Florida

In Tallahassee, Florida, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.93 as of 2026-03-24 — $0.48 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,485, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.58% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Tallahassee's cost-of-living index of 88 puts it below the national average of 100.
