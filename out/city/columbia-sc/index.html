Columbia, South Carolina

In Columbia, South Carolina, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.65 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.20 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,243.38, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.88% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Columbia's cost-of-living index of 82 puts it below the national average of 100.
