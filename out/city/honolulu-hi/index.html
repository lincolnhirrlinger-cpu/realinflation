Honolulu, Hawaii

In Honolulu, Hawaii, a gallon of regular gas costs $5.23 as of 2026-03-23 — $1.78 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $4,319.7, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.3% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Honolulu's cost-of-living index of 175 puts it above the national average of 100.
