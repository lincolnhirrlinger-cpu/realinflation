Baton Rouge, Louisiana

In Baton Rouge, Louisiana, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.58 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.13 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,150, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.34% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Baton Rouge's cost-of-living index of 88 puts it below the national average of 100.
