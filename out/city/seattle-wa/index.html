Seattle, Washington

In Seattle, Washington, a gallon of regular gas costs $5.27 as of 2026-03-23 — $1.82 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $2,400, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.58% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Seattle's cost-of-living index of 122 puts it above the national average of 100.
