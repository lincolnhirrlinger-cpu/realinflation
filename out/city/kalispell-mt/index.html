Kalispell, Montana

In Kalispell, Montana, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.70 as of 2026-03-27 — $0.27 below the national average of $3.98. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $2,172, compared to the national average of $1,895. Grocery inflation is running at 5.27% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Kalispell's cost-of-living index of 100 puts it below the national average of 100.
