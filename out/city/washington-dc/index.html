Washington, District of Columbia

In Washington, District of Columbia, a gallon of regular gas costs $4.07 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.62 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $2,305.1, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.75% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Washington's cost-of-living index of 155 puts it above the national average of 100.
