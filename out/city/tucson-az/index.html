Tucson, Arizona

In Tucson, Arizona, a gallon of regular gas costs $4.61 as of 2026-03-24 — $0.63 above the national average of $3.98. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,447, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 5.27% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Tucson's cost-of-living index of 90 puts it below the national average of 100.
