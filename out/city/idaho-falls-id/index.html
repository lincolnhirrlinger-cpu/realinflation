Idaho Falls, Idaho

In Idaho Falls, Idaho, a gallon of regular gas costs $4.05 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.60 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,100, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 3.06% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Idaho Falls's cost-of-living index of 90 puts it below the national average of 100.
