Salt Lake City, Utah

In Salt Lake City, Utah, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.94 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.49 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,758.93, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 3.26% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Salt Lake City's cost-of-living index of 108 puts it above the national average of 100.
