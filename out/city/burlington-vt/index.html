Burlington, Vermont

In Burlington, Vermont, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.90 as of 2026-03-25 — $0.08 below the national average of $3.98. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $2,144, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 5.27% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Burlington's cost-of-living index of 112 puts it above the national average of 100.
