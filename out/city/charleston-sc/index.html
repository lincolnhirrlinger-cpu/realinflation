Charleston, South Carolina

In Charleston, South Carolina, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.67 as of 2026-03-26 — $0.31 below the national average of $3.98. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,969, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 5.27% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Charleston's cost-of-living index of 98 puts it below the national average of 100.
