Atlanta, Georgia

In Atlanta, Georgia, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.67 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.22 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,523.61, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.09% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Atlanta's cost-of-living index of 110 puts it above the national average of 100.
