Montpelier, Vermont

In Montpelier, Vermont, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.86 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.41 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $2,131.06, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 3.27% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Montpelier's cost-of-living index of 98 puts it below the national average of 100.
