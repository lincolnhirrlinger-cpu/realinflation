Miami, Florida

In Miami, Florida, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.93 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.48 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,990.17, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 3.13% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Miami's cost-of-living index of 130 puts it above the national average of 100.
