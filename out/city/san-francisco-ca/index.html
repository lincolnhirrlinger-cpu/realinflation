San Francisco, California

In San Francisco, California, a gallon of regular gas costs $5.82 as of 2026-03-24 — $1.85 above the national average of $3.98. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $3,103, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 5.27% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. San Francisco's cost-of-living index of 145 puts it above the national average of 100.
