Madison, Wisconsin

In Madison, Wisconsin, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.62 as of 2026-03-31 — $0.40 below the national average of $4.02. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,659, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 5.27% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Madison's cost-of-living index of 100 puts it below the national average of 100.
