Portland, Maine

In Portland, Maine, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.80 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.35 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,600, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.58% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Portland's cost-of-living index of 105 puts it above the national average of 100.
