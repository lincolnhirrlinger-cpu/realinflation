Raleigh, North Carolina

In Raleigh, North Carolina, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.73 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.28 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,600, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.19% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Raleigh's cost-of-living index of 100 puts it below the national average of 100.
