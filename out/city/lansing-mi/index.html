Lansing, Michigan

In Lansing, Michigan, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.92 as of 2026-03-23 — $0.47 above the national average of $3.45. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,100, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.57% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Lansing's cost-of-living index of 80 puts it below the national average of 100.
