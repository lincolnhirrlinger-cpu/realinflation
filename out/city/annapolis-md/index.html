Annapolis, Maryland

In Annapolis, Maryland, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.88 as of 2026-03-24 — $0.42 above the national average of $3.45. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks.
