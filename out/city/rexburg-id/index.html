Rexburg, Idaho

In Rexburg, Idaho, a gallon of regular gas costs $3.72 as of 2026-03-22 — $0.16 below the national average of $3.88. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $845, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 1.7% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Rexburg's cost-of-living index of 82 puts it below the national average of 100.
