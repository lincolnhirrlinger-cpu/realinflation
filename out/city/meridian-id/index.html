Meridian, Idaho

In Meridian, Idaho, a gallon of regular gas costs $4.17 as of 2026-03-22 — $0.29 above the national average of $3.88. The average monthly rent for all unit types is $1,550, compared to the national average of $1,900. Grocery inflation is running at 2.58% year-over-year. Since 2022, bread prices have increased significantly while egg prices have been volatile due to avian flu outbreaks. Meridian's cost-of-living index of 108 puts it above the national average of 100.
